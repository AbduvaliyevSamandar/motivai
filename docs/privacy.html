Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MotivAI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account data: name, email address, password (hashed), profile picture
  • Authentication: Google account info if you sign in with Google (email, name, profile picture, Google user ID)
  • User-generated content: tasks, plans, goals, habits, flashcards, chat messages with the AI assistant, journal entries (morning ritual)

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: XP, streak, completed tasks count, focus minutes
  • Device info (locally only): theme preference, sound pack, haptic settings

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app's functionality
  • Generate AI-powered task suggestions and motivational content
  • Track your progress (XP, streaks, leaderboard)
  • Send notifications and reminders you've configured
  • Verify your email address via one-time codes (OTP)
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and spam (e.g., disposable email blocking)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate MotivAI:

  • Google Sign-In: for optional authentication. Google's privacy policy
  • OpenAI API: AI chat and task suggestions. Messages may be sent to OpenAI's servers. OpenAI privacy
  • MongoDB Atlas: encrypted database hosting
  • Render.com: server hosting
  • Gmail SMTP: sending verification emails

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text
  • Authentication tokens use industry-standard JWT with HS256
  • OTP codes are hashed in the database and expire in 10 minutes
  • Connections to our servers use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Local app data (preferences, cached tasks) is stored on your device only

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the app:

  • Profile → "Akkauntni o'chirish" (Delete account)
  • This removes your user record, all tasks/plans, chat history, leaderboard entries, and progress data
  • Deletion is immediate and irreversible

6. Children

MotivAI is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, contact us and we will delete the account.

7. International Users

Our servers are located in the United States and Europe (via Render and MongoDB Atlas). By using MotivAI, you consent to your data being processed in those regions.

8. Your Rights

  • Access — view your data via the app or by exporting (Profile → "Ma'lumotlarni eksport")
  • Rectification — update your profile, change password
  • Deletion — delete your account from within the app
  • Portability — export your data as JSON

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Major changes will be announced in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
